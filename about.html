Our Story

At Casa Designs, we trade mass production for the steady rhythm of our wood shop. By pairing with intentional design with time-honored techniques, we create custom surfaces that respect both the chef and the timber. It's craftsmanship you can feel every time the knife hits the wood.

Located in Tualatin, Oregon, Casa Designs is a custom woodworking studio specializing in handcrafted cutting boards, charcuterie boards, cheese slicers, and custom furniture pieces. With a passion for quality craftsmanship and a commitment to using sustainable materials, we create unique and functional pieces that bring warmth and beauty to any space. Whether you're looking for a one-of-a-kind cutting board or a custom furniture piece, Casa Designs is dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service to every client.

The Materials We Use

At Casa Designs, materials drive the work. From the hardwoods we source to the finishes we apply, every decision is made with longevity, safety, and performance in mind. We select premium hardwoods for their durability and beauty, ensuring that each piece not only looks stunning but also stands the test of time. Our food-safe oils and finishes are carefully chosen to enhance the natural beauty of the wood while providing a protective barrier against moisture and wear. With proper care and maintenance, our products will continue to shine in your kitchen for years to come.

We work with carefully selected, premium hardwoods chosen for their strength, character, and long-term performance. Each species brings its own grain pattern, hardness, and personality - and we select materiables based on how they will live and age in a space.
