Who We Are

Casa Designs is located in Tualatin, Oregon. All woodwork is handcrafted in my home shop with a small variety of available inventory.

A large potion of my work is custom made cutting boards based on specific dimensions, choice of wood and its intended use or location. These include Counter Top, Stove Top, Under Counter Insert, and Sink Inserts.

Charcuterie boards and Cheese Slicers are also made with the same craftsmanship, care, hardwoods, and food-safe preparation.

I have been commissioned to provide unique wood projects for restaurant center pieces and buffet displays, various pro shop and course projects for the SF Olympic Club Golf Club, and many other custom projects like personalized boxes and gifts.

Experience & Creativity

With decades of hands-on experience and an artist’s eye for detail, Casa Designs transforms simple hardwoods into functional pieces of art.

Premium Hardwoods & Finishes

Only the highest quality woods are sourced for each project—think walnut, maple, cherry, and exotic grains—finished with professional-grade oils and treatments for durability and beauty.

One Craftsman, Start to Finish

Every piece is personally crafted in a small Tualatin workshop by my hands. From design to delivery, there's no handoff—just dedication.

Functional, Custom-Tailored Design

From sizing to wood type to finish, every detail is built around your space and lifestyle. These are not just cutting boards—they’re heirlooms in the making.

Meet the Craftsman

Jack LaBue

Jack is the hands, heart, and hard-earned experience behind Casa Designs. From his workshop in Tualatin, Oregon, he crafts every piece with passion and precision.

As a retired craftsman, Jack dedicates his time to creating one-of-a-kind woodworking projects — from custom cutting boards to commissioned custom pieces — each telling its own story of craftsmanship and care.
